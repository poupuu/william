Our Story

Crafting Moments Since 2010

In the heart of a bustling city, Susanto Liman dreamed of creating a space where coffee wasn't just a beverage, but an experience that brought people together. With a background in culinary arts and a passion for coffee cultivation, Susanto embarked on a journey to remote mountaintops, sourcing the finest beans directly from farmers.

"Coffee is more than caffeine; it's connection. At MR.COFFEE, we don't just serve drinks; we craft moments."
